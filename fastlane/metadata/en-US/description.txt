- Download/Import image from other apps (Browser, SNS, Photo App, e.g. Safari, Twitter, Photo) and manage it with meta data (Web Site url, data size...etc).
- Collect multiple images in a clip and manage it.
- You can add clips to albums.
- You can add tags and classify clips by it.
- You can search your clips by url, tags, and albums.
- You can hides clips, tags, albums from everywhere by display setting.
- Support iCloud Sync.
